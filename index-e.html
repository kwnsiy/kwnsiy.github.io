Seiya KawanoDr.Eng. — 河野 誠也

Assistant Professor (tenure-track) at the Kyoto Institute of Technology (Faculty of Information and Human Sciences). I study dialogue intelligence that elucidates the mechanisms of knowledge acquisition and social relationship building in human-human and human-robot dialogue, and computationally supports them.
