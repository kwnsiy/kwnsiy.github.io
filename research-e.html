Research Topics

User-Adaptive Spoken Dialogue Systems and Dialogue Robots

Toward systems that can converse naturally with humans, I work on response generation considering dialogue context and structure, modeling of entrainment and style-shifting, rapport-building dialogue strategies, and LLM-driven motion control for dialogue robots.

Analyzing and Enhancing the Understanding and Reasoning of LLMs

I study language understanding and generation centered on large language models, including enhancing the understanding of implied meanings in LLMs based on pragmatic theories, and training dialogue systems with AI feedback.

Patent and Intellectual Property Informatics

I develop natural language processing that supports the decision-making of intellectual property practitioners: discourse structure analysis of patent documents, automated patent claim refinement, prior-art search support, and cross-lingual multi-jurisdiction datasets for patent drafting.

Multimodal Dialogue and Real-World Interaction

I build multimodal dialogue corpora integrating nonverbal signals such as gaze, facial expressions, and body motion (including event cameras), and study real-world reference resolution and visual question answering for dialogue intelligence that assists people in real environments.
